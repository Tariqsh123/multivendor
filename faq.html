Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about GlobalMart platform, ordering, shipping, and account management.

General Questions

What is GlobalMart?

GlobalMart is a multi-vendor B2B and B2C marketplace that connects Chinese suppliers with global markets. Our platform enables shippers to upload products to warehouses, store managers to curate stores, and customers to shop from multiple vendors in one place.

How does the three-tier model work?

Our unique three-tier model includes:
1. Shippers: Upload products to our warehouse with pricing and commission rates
2. Store Managers: Browse warehouse products and add them to their stores with markup
3. Customers: Shop from all stores, add to cart, and checkout seamlessly

Is GlobalMart free to use?

Yes, GlobalMart is completely free for customers to browse and shop. For shippers and store managers, there are no upfront fees. Shippers earn commissions on sales, and store managers earn through markup on products sold from their stores.

Account & Registration

How do I create an account?

Click on "Account" in the navigation menu, then select "Sign Up". Choose your role (Customer, Store Manager, or Shipper), fill in your details, and click "Create Account". You'll be automatically logged in and redirected to your dashboard.

Can I change my role after registration?

Currently, you cannot change your role after registration. Each role has specific permissions and dashboards. If you want to operate in a different role, you'll need to create a separate account with a different email address.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and we'll send you a password reset link. For this demo version, please contact support if you need assistance with account recovery.

How do store managers set up their store?

After registering as a store manager, go to your dashboard. You can set your store name, browse warehouse products, and add them to your store. You'll set your own prices (with markup) and manage your store inventory from the dashboard.

Ordering & Payments

How do I place an order?

1. Browse products and click "Add to Cart"
2. Click the cart icon to review your items
3. Click "Proceed to Checkout"
4. Enter shipping and payment information
5. Click "Place Order" to complete your purchase

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Stripe. For B2B transactions, we also offer bank transfer options (contact support for details).

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, all payments are processed through secure payment gateways. We use SSL encryption to protect your personal and payment information. GlobalMart does not store your credit card details on our servers.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Orders can be cancelled or modified within 1 hour of placement if they haven't been processed for shipping. Go to your dashboard > My Orders to cancel. For modifications, contact our support team immediately.

Shipping & Delivery

Where does GlobalMart ship to?

We ship worldwide to over 50 countries. During checkout, you can select your country from the dropdown menu. Some remote locations may have shipping restrictions.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 7-14 business days for most destinations. Express shipping (3-7 business days) is available for an additional fee. Shipping times may vary based on destination and customs clearance.

What are the shipping costs?

Standard shipping is Rs 1,677 for most orders (based on $5.99 × 280). Free shipping is available for orders over Rs 28,000. Express shipping costs vary by destination and are calculated at checkout.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also view tracking information in your dashboard under "My Orders".

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be unused, in original packaging with all tags attached. Shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective or incorrect.
