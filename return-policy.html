Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following criteria:

Eligible for Return

  • Timeframe: Within 30 days of delivery date
  • Condition: Unused, unworn, and in original packaging with all tags attached
  • Proof of Purchase: Original receipt or order confirmation required
  • Reasons Accepted: Defective, damaged, incorrect item, or size exchange
  • Original Packaging: All original boxes, manuals, and accessories included

Non-Returnable Items

  • Personalized or custom-made products
  • Intimate apparel (underwear, swimwear) for hygiene reasons
  • Opened software, digital products, or downloadable content
  • Gift cards and store credits
  • Products marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"
  • Items damaged due to misuse or improper care
  • Perishable goods and food items

Special Considerations

  • Electronics: Must include all accessories and be factory reset
  • Fragile Items: Must be packed securely to prevent damage during return shipping
  • Multi-item Sets: All pieces must be returned together
  • Sale Items: Final sale items cannot be returned unless defective

Detailed Return Instructions

  1. Log into your GlobalMart account and go to "My Orders"
  2. Select the item you wish to return and click "Return Item"
  3. Choose your return reason from the dropdown menu
  4. Wait for approval (usually within 24 hours)
  5. Print your return label from the email we send you
  6. Pack the item securely in its original packaging with all accessories
  7. Include the completed return form inside the package
  8. Attach the return label to the outside of the package
  9. Drop off at authorized carrier location and keep the tracking number

Important Packaging Instructions

Please pack items securely using the original packaging when possible. For items without original packaging, use sturdy boxes with adequate padding. We cannot accept responsibility for items damaged during return shipping due to improper packaging.

Refund Amounts

  • Full Refund: For defective, damaged, or incorrect items (including original shipping cost)
  • Partial Refund: For items returned in used condition (minus restocking fee of 15%)
  • No Refund: For items not meeting return criteria or outside return window
  • Exchange: Size/color exchanges are free (customer pays return shipping for non-defective items)

Deductions & Fees

  • Restocking Fee: 15% for opened but undamaged items
  • Return Shipping: Customer responsibility for non-defective items
  • Original Shipping: Non-refundable for changed mind returns
  • Customs/Duties: Non-refundable for international returns

Exchange Process

  1. Initiate an exchange request through your dashboard
  2. Specify preferred size/color (subject to availability)
  3. Receive prepaid return label (free for defective/wrong items)
  4. Ship item back to us
  5. We ship replacement once return is received
  6. Receive tracking information for new item

Exchange Notes

Availability: Exchanges are subject to stock availability. If your preferred item is out of stock, we'll issue a refund or store credit.
Price Differences: If exchanging for a higher-priced item, you'll pay the difference. For lower-priced items, you'll receive store credit for the difference.
International Exchanges: Additional shipping and customs fees may apply.

International Returns

For customers outside our primary shipping regions:

Key Differences

  • Extended Return Window: 45 days for international customers
  • Return Shipping: Customer responsibility (prepaid labels not available internationally)
  • Customs Documentation: Must mark as "Returned Goods" to avoid duties
  • Refund Processing: Additional 5-7 business days for international refunds
  • Currency Conversion: Refunds in original currency, conversion fees may apply

Important Notes for International Returns

  • Use a trackable shipping method and retain proof of postage
  • Declare package value as $0 and mark as "Returned Goods" on customs form
  • We cannot reimburse original customs/duties paid
  • Refunds minus original shipping costs and any return shipping costs
  • Allow additional time for customs clearance in both directions

Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive a defective or damaged item, we'll make it right:

Our Commitment

  • Free Return Shipping: Prepaid label provided
  • Full Refund: Including original shipping costs
  • Priority Processing: Expedited handling
  • Replacement Option: If available in stock
  • Extended Window: 90 days for manufacturing defects

What to Do If You Receive a Defective Item

  1. Document the Issue: Take clear photos/videos showing the defect or damage
  2. Contact Us Immediately: Within 48 hours of delivery for damaged items
  3. Do Not Use/Discard: Keep all packaging and do not attempt repairs
  4. Submit Documentation: Through your dashboard or email to support
  5. Follow Instructions: We'll guide you through the specific return process

Documentation Requirements

For defective/damaged items, we require:
- Clear photos of the defect/damage
- Photos of packaging and shipping labels
- Video showing the issue (for electronics)
- Copy of your order confirmation
This documentation helps us process your claim faster and improve our quality control.
