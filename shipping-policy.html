Shipping Cost Calculation

Shipping costs are calculated based on:

  • Destination Country: Different regions have different base rates
  • Package Weight & Dimensions: Calculated automatically at checkout
  • Shipping Method: Standard, Express, or Expedited
  • Order Value: Free standard shipping on orders over Rs 28,000

Important Note

Shipping costs for multiple items from different stores are calculated separately, as items ship directly from different warehouses. We're working to consolidate shipping for better customer experience.

Factors Affecting Delivery Times

  • Customs Clearance: International shipments must clear customs, which can add 1-5 days
  • Weather Conditions: Severe weather may cause delays
  • Holiday Seasons: Delivery times may increase during peak seasons
  • Remote Locations: Rural areas may have longer delivery times
  • Product Availability: Items from different warehouses ship separately

Order Tracking

We provide comprehensive tracking for all shipments so you can monitor your order's journey from our warehouse to your doorstep.

Tracking Information

Once your order ships, you'll receive:

  1. Shipping confirmation email with tracking number
  2. Real-time tracking updates via email or SMS (optional)
  3. Delivery notification when your package is out for delivery
  4. Delivery confirmation once your package is delivered

How to Track Your Order

You can track your order using any of these methods:

  • Dashboard: Log into your account and visit "My Orders"
  • Tracking Link: Click the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
  • Carrier Website: Use the tracking number on the carrier's website
  • Mobile App: Use our mobile app (coming soon) for push notifications

Common Tracking Statuses

  • Label Created: Shipping label generated, awaiting pickup
  • In Transit: Package is moving through the carrier network
  • Out for Delivery: Package is on the delivery vehicle
  • Delivered: Package has been delivered
  • Customs Clearance: Package is undergoing customs inspection

International Shipping

As a global marketplace, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Here's what you need to know about international shipping:

Customs & Duties

International shipments may be subject to:

  • Import Duties: Taxes imposed by the destination country
  • Customs Fees: Administrative fees for customs processing
  • Value Added Tax (VAT): Applicable in many countries

Important: GlobalMart is not responsible for any customs fees, import duties, or taxes that may be applied to your order. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and vary by country.

Restricted & Prohibited Items

Some items cannot be shipped internationally due to:

  • Country-specific import restrictions
  • Hazardous materials regulations
  • Size and weight limitations
  • Cultural or religious restrictions

Customs Declaration

All international shipments include proper customs documentation with accurate product descriptions and values. Please ensure your shipping address is complete and accurate to avoid customs delays.

Shipping Issues & Solutions

Common Issues

  • Delivery Delays: Contact us if your package is significantly delayed
  • Incorrect Address: Update your address within 1 hour of ordering
  • Package Not Received: Wait 2 business days past estimated delivery date
  • Damaged Package: Document damage and contact us immediately
  • Lost Tracking: Contact support if tracking stops updating

What to Do If...

Your package is delayed: Check tracking first, then contact our support team after 2 business days past the estimated delivery date.

You received the wrong item: Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the item received and packing slip.

The package is damaged: Take photos of the damaged package and contents, then contact us immediately.

You need to change the delivery address: Contact us immediately if the order hasn't shipped. Once shipped, address changes may not be possible.
